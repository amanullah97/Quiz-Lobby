Why QuizLobby?

With the advancement of a digital era, our educational system has also taken a step forward; the schools and universities are now conducting online classes, especially since the Covid’19 has started.

The schools and universities are now conducting online classes, especially since the Covid’19 has started. However, our teachers, in most parts of the world, are still doing their work manually, leading to a hectic routine. They don't have automated platforms to conduct quizzes professionally.

This is where our QuizLobby project will come in handy, to propose a solution and provide a platform to our teachers. The QuizLobby community will help Teachers by giving them a web platform where they can easily create a quiz according to their requirements and conduct it online.
